Mengchu Li

I'm a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Statistics at the University of Warwick. I will join the School of Mathematics at the University of Birmingham, as an Assistant Professor of Statistics in October.

Previously, I obtained my PhD in Statistics from the University of Warwick in 2023, master's degree in Mathematical Statistics from the University of Cambridge in 2019, and bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Economics from Durham University in 2018.

Research Interests: change point analysis, high-dimensional statistics, robust statistics and differential privacy.
