Mengchu Li

I'm currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics, University of Birmingham. Here is my University webpage.

From February 2023 to Septemeber 2024, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Statistics, University of Warwick. In April 2023, I defended my PhD thesis which I worked on under the supervision of Professor Yi Yu at the University of Warwick.

Research Interests: change point analysis, high-dimensional statistics, robust statistics and differential privacy.
